What is it ?

A simple, beautiful and fast startpage that lets you access your bookmarks and search your favourite web services, all that with just a few clicks. Replace your browsers default startpage with a truly beautiful startpage, that focuses on simplicity and productivity.

Why does it rock ?

Unlike any other startpage, That Startpage Rocks focuses on simplicity and productivity. It's a truly distraction-free startpage that is also beautiful, due to its modern interface.

Add bookmarks

Thanks to our icon repository, add bookmarks that have beautiful icons.

Quickly search websites

Find content from major websites easily with our powerfull search engine. For example, just type youtube followed by a query and you will get realtime results showing video links.

Here are some of our supported websites :

  • Youtube
  • Spotify
  • Soundcloud
  • Wikipedia
  • .. And more !

Customize your startpage

With our editor, you can easily customize your startpage's background, choose a theme and more. Make that startpage yours ! Make it rock !

Save your startpage locally

Save your startpage on your computer to have an even faster experience.
