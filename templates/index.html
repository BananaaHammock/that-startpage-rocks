What is it ?

A simple, beautiful and quick start page that lets you access your bookmarks and search your favourite web services, all that with just a click or a few keystrokes. It's a perfect replacement to the default startpage on your browser.

Why does it rock ?

Compared other startpages available on the web, That Startpage Rocks focuses on simplicity and productivity. Our aim is that you spend the least time finding what you want. No distractions are there to slow you down, just powerful tools that help you quickly find the content you want. We also focus on design. The modern interface and the customizability of our platform let you create the most unique and beautiful startpage.

Add your favourite websites

Like you would expect, add your favourite websites to your startpage. Thanks to our icon repository, your apps have beautifully sharp icons.

Search famous websites

Our very special search engine lets you quickly find content from major web services. It works a little bit like a command line. For instance, if you'd like to search a youtube video, just type youtube followed by a query. You'll then get realtime results, linking directly to videos.

Search for content on from these websites :

  • Youtube
  • Spotify
  • Soundcloud
  • Wikipedia
  • .. And more !

Customize your startpage

Change your startpage's look and feel with our simple interface, which lets you choose a colour theme, change your startpage's name or change your startpage's background.
