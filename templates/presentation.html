Startpage, n. 1. The opening page in a browser, set by the user. Also known as homepage.

How it works

First, signup. Then, follow a few steps to customize your startpage. And that's it ! We give you a link to your startpage, from which you can access your bookmarks and search. To add or remove bookmarks, or to customize your startpage's look, place your cursor on the button (on the top right corner of your startpage), and click on 'edit'.

Quickly find the right content

A Powerful search engine

One can compare it to a terminal, as if you send commands to the search input and get results specific to these commands. Now don't be afraid by this, it's actually very simple, it's also super quick. Our search engine lets you search directly through major web services to quickly access the content you want. Say you'd like to listen to a song on spotify, write spotify or spot followed by your query and you get direct links to songs.
